About Me

Saya Reehan Putra Perdana, lahir di Surakarta, 25 september 2001 saat ini sebagai mahasiswa tingkat semester 5 jurusan Teknik Komputer di Universitas Diponegoro Semarang. Kegiatan saya saat ini adalah berkuliah seperti biasa sembari meningkatkan pengetahuan saya di bidang IT dan desain.

Exploration is my way to develop myself independently. Effort and prayer are my priorities in working and studying, so complaining is not a word that can be accepted by me personally. Sometimes life doesn't give you what you want, not because you don't deserve it, but because you deserve so much more.
